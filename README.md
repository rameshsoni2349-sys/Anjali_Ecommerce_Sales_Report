## 5. Profit by Month (Column Chart)

**Insight:**

👉 Profit changes every month.
👉 **January, March, October, and December** show higher profit.
👉 Some months have lower profit and need improvement.

---

## 6. Profit by Sub-Category (Bar Chart)

**Insight:**

👉 **Printers** give the highest profit.
👉 **Bookcases** and **Sarees** also perform well.
👉 **Tables** and **Trousers** generate less profit.

---

## 7. Payment Mode by Sub-Category (Donut Chart)

**Insight:**

* **COD (45.6%)** → Most used payment method.
* **UPI (22.07%)** → Second most used.
* **Debit Card (13.47%)**
* **Credit Card (10.87%)**

👉 Customers prefer **Cash on Delivery (COD)**.

---

## 8. Quantity by Sub-Category

**Insight:**

👉 Some sub-categories sell more products than others.
👉 Higher quantity means higher customer demand.

---

## 9. Amount by Customer Name (Bar Chart)

**Insight:**

👉 **Harivansh** purchased the highest amount.
👉 **Madhav, Madan Mohan, and Shiva** are also top customers.

👉 These are the company's valuable customers.

---

## 10. Amount by State (Bar Chart)

**Insight:**

👉 **Maharashtra** generated the highest sales amount.
👉 **Madhya Pradesh** is second.
👉 **Uttar Pradesh** and **Delhi** contributed less sales.

---

## 11. Quarter Slicer (Qtr 1, Qtr 2, Qtr 3, Qtr 4)

**Insight:**

👉 Used to filter dashboard data by quarter.
👉 Helps compare sales and profit across different quarters.

---

# Overall Dashboard Insight

👉 Total Sales = **438K**

👉 **COD** is the most popular payment mode.
